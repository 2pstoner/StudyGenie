StudyGenie: The Revolutionary Learning Platform - Making Education Fun and Affordable!

Who would have thought a group of computer science students from Pwani University would create a website like StudyGenie? We must have been studying too much, because we came up with a platform that offers a wide range of resources for university students, including tutorial videos, past papers, audiobooks, book summaries, and other documents that can be accessed and downloaded for free. And let's not forget our paid services where we help with assignments and offer consultation. But don't worry, we won't charge an arm and a leg, just a small fee that goes towards our volunteers and shareholders. Who says studying can't be fun? With StudyGenie, we're making education a party!

StudyGenie is a Collaborative Learning Platform for University Students, Developed by Computer Science Students from Pwani University and Supported by Education Professionals from Universities Across Kenya
